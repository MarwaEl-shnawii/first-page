Tom yum Seafood

a famous Thai delicacy known for its rich and spicy flavors. It combines fresh seafood like shrimp and squid with a tangy, aromatic broth made from lemongrass, lime, and chili, creating a perfect balance of heat and sourness.

Seafood Paella

traditional Spanish recipe originating from Valencia. It features a flavorful mix of saffron-infused rice, fresh seafood such as shrimp, mussels, and squid, along with vegetables and spices, cooked together in a wide, shallow pan.
